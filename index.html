About Me

Turning vision into
visual reality

I'm a highly experienced ROTO SUPERVISOR and VFX Artist with a strong background in delivering high-quality visual effects for feature films and streaming productions. With nearly a decade of industry experience, I have progressed from a Roto Artist to leading teams and supervising complex VFX workflows across multiple studios in India..

I specialize in rotoscoping, paint, compositing support, and team leadership, ensuring precision, consistency, and efficiency across large-scale productions. My expertise includes working with industry-standard tools such as Nuke, Silhouette, and Mocha, enabling me to handle complex shots with accuracy and speed.

I bring strong skills in team management, pipeline coordination, and project delivery under tight deadlines, consistently ensuring that quality and timelines are met. Having worked in studios like NY VFXWAALA, Prime Focus, Cinegence Media, Silvercloud Studios, and Fractal Pictures, I have built a solid foundation in both technical execution and leadership.
